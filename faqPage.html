How do I register for online banking?
  • Visit your bank’s official website or download their mobile app.
  • Click "Register" or "Sign Up" and enter your account details (account number, debit card, etc.).
  • Verify your identity via OTP (one-time password) or security questions, then set up login credentials.
Is online banking safe?
  • Yes, banks use encryption, two-factor authentication (2FA), and secure servers.
  • Always use strong passwords, avoid public Wi-Fi, and enable fraud alerts for extra security.
Can I transfer money to another bank using online banking?
  • Yes, select "Transfer" or "Send Money" in your online banking portal.
  • Enter the recipient’s account details (IFSC code, account number).
  • Confirm the transaction with an OTP or authentication code.
